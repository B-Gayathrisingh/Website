POP Wiring

POP wiring involves concealed electrical wiring done inside plaster of Paris (POP) ceilings and walls for a clean, modern finish. Our skilled electricians at Emergency Electricals specialize in safe and organized POP wiring that keeps your space looking neat while ensuring long-lasting performance. We use high-quality materials and follow safety standards to prevent overheating or electrical hazards.

  • Concealed wiring inside false ceilings and walls
  • Clean, modern finish without visible wires
  • Safe installation with quality fire-resistant cables
  • Ideal for homes, offices, and commercial spaces
  • Custom layout planning for lighting & fixtures
